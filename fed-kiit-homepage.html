Who We Are

The Official Student Body of KIIT TBI

Founded under KIIT's Technology Business Incubator, FED exists to bring all ideas and potential startups under one umbrella by providing resources, connects, and mentors. We also organise gatherings to spread entrepreneurship-related awareness.

Our goal is simple: make entrepreneurship accessible to every student at KIIT, regardless of background or stream.

🎯

Mission

Empower students to ideate, validate, and build ventures that create real-world impact.

👁️

Vision

To be the leading student entrepreneurship ecosystem in Eastern India, fostering unicorn founders of tomorrow.

💎

Values

Innovation, Collaboration, Resilience, Integrity — we build with purpose and execute with passion.
